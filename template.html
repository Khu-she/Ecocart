Template Page

This is a template showing how to use reusable navbar and footer components.

How to Use:
  1. Keep the <div id="navbar-placeholder"></div> at the top of your page
  2. Keep the <div id="footer-placeholder"></div> before the closing body tag
  3. Include the navbar.js and footer.js scripts at the bottom
  4. The components will automatically load and detect the active page!
